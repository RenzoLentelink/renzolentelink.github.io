Tanjiro Kamado - Info

----------

Background

Tanjiro Kamado is the eldest son in a family of charcoal sellers. He lives a simple and happy life in the mountains with his mother and siblings. Tanjiro is known for his kind-hearted nature and his strong sense of responsibility, often taking on the role of the family's breadwinner after his father's passing.

Tragic Events

Tanjiro's life takes a drastic turn when he returns home one day to find his family slaughtered by demons, with only his sister, Nezuko, surviving the attack. However, Nezuko has been transformed into a demon. Determined to find a cure for his sister and avenge his family, Tanjiro joins the Demon Slayer Corps, a secret organization dedicated to protecting humanity from demons.

Personality

Tanjiro is characterized by his unwavering kindness, empathy, and determination. He possesses a strong sense of justice and is deeply compassionate, even towards demons, understanding that they were once human. His resolve to protect his loved ones and his resilience in the face of adversity make him a natural leader and an inspiring figure among his peers.
